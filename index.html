CSS Trainer

Introduction

Dear User,

thank you for choosing my CSS-Trainer. I hope you will like my creation, but what's most important to me is that this Application will help you become a true CSS-Expert. I made sure that all Tasks that are waiting for you, are made in such a way that you neither get overwhelmed nor underchallenged and that they all cover the most important CSS-Rules you won't get around when developing Websites or Single-Page-Applications. I know for well that my Application is not perfect, so in case you find any Bugs or have Improvement Suggestions, feel free to hit me on GitHub: https://github.com/Nosgard

So without further hesitation, go prove your CSS-Skills!

How to Use

Basics

On Top of every Task, you have:

1. The Exercise telling you what you have to do

2. The Preview of the affected Elements

The Tasks

To solve a Task, the following Editors are provided:

The HTML-Editor

- Gives you the HTML-Structure you need to solve the Task

The CSS-Editor

- Gives you a Template of a CSS-Rule, where you can enter the missing parts. After you have done that, simply click the Check-Button to validate your Answer
